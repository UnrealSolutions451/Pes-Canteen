About PES Canteen

Who We Are

PES Canteen is the official digital canteen ordering platform for P.E.S. College of Engineering, Ch. Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad). This platform allows students and staff to conveniently browse the menu, place food orders, and make secure online payments through integrated payment gateways.

The platform is owned and managed by the college to enhance the on-campus dining experience with technology-driven convenience, transparency, and efficiency.

Our Mission

Our mission is to digitize campus services by providing an efficient, cashless, and student-friendly food ordering system. With PES Canteen, we aim to reduce waiting times, enhance service management, and promote transparency in billing and transactions.
